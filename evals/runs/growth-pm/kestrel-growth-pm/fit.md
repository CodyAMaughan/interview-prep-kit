## The meta-move (per strong line)
- **Experimentation program:** I ran ~60 experiments/year owning the onboarding/activation roadmap → the textbook approach is a fixed-horizon A/B test sized by MDE → I leaned on CUPED and sequential testing to get reads faster on a high-velocity backlog, accepting the added analytical complexity for velocity.
- **Ship/no-ship from data:** I shipped the onboarding variant that lifted D7 22→28% over a prettier variant that tested flat → the standard move is to ship the highest-measured-lift arm → I held to the success metric I'd pre-defined and chose retention impact over aesthetic appeal, owning the call.
- **Guardrails / killing bad ideas:** leadership wanted a mandatory tutorial → the standard is to ship a leadership-favored bet or argue it in a meeting → I ran a holdout instead; it dropped activation 6% and the data ended the debate.
- **Metric definition:** a VP wanted to optimize invites-sent → the easy path is to report the metric asked for → I argued invites-sent is a vanity metric and rebuilt the readout around activated-invitees, which changed which variants shipped.
- **Measurable funnel impact:** I built the LTV model at Cobalt → the standard is last-click ROAS → I modeled lifetime value and reallocated ~$1.5M of spend on that basis.

## Honest framing
This is a **resurfacing-plus-stretch** profile, not a learning-from-scratch one. The experimentation, analytics, SQL, and measurable-funnel-impact half of the job is a **direct strength the candidate already does at PM altitude** — they own a roadmap (for experiments), define success metrics, and make ship calls. The genuine stretch is the **product-craft half**: owning a full product roadmap and backlog across a quarter, writing full PRDs, and quarterly planning. The candidate should name this explicitly in the room — "I've done slices of the PM job: the success-metric and ship-decision slices deeply, the full-roadmap and PRD slices not yet" — and lean on the fact that the role is *experimentation-heavy*, so they're strongest exactly where this role is weighted most.
