# Job description — Senior Backend Engineer, Payments Platform

> Modeled on real public senior-backend/payments postings. Fictional company "Meridian Pay".
> Pairs with persona `backend-strong`.

**Meridian Pay — Senior Backend Engineer, Payments Platform**
Remote (US) · Payments Platform team

## About the role
Meridian processes billions of dollars in payments for online businesses. The Payments Platform team
owns the ledger, money movement, and the APIs that every product and merchant integration is built on.
We're hiring a senior backend engineer to own core services and raise the reliability bar.

## What you'll do
- Design, build, and operate high-throughput, low-latency backend services for money movement and
  ledgering, with strong correctness and reliability guarantees.
- Own data integrity: idempotency, reconciliation, and exactly-once semantics under failure and retry.
- Set technical direction — write design docs, review designs, and mentor other engineers.
- Carry the reliability of what you own: alerting, on-call, runbooks, incident response.

## Required
- 5+ years building backend services in production.
- Deep experience with relational databases (Postgres/MySQL) and data-modeling for correctness.
- Strong distributed-systems fundamentals: partitioning, replication, consistency trade-offs, idempotency.
- Fluent in one of Go / Java / Python.
- A track record of owning reliability for a production service.

## Nice to have
- Payments, ledgering, or other correctness-critical financial systems experience.
- Kubernetes in production; infrastructure as code.
- Experience building platform/API abstractions used by other teams.

## Interview process
1. Recruiter screen (30 min, non-technical).
2. Technical screen (60 min): coding + a systems/data-modeling discussion.
3. Onsite (virtual): system design, a deep-dive on past work, and a values/leadership conversation.
