# Job description — Data Scientist, Product & Growth

> Modeled on real public data-scientist postings that require production ML. Fictional company "Helio".
> Pairs with persona `analyst-to-ds`.

**Helio — Data Scientist, Product & Growth**
Hybrid (Chicago) · Data Science team

## About the role
Helio is a consumer subscription product. The Data Science team partners with product and growth to
build models and experiments that drive retention and monetization. We're hiring a data scientist who
can both run rigorous experiments and ship models to production.

## What you'll do
- Design and analyze A/B experiments end to end; advise product on causal reads, not just dashboards.
- Build, deploy, and monitor ML models in production (churn, propensity, LTV) — own them past the
  notebook through serving, monitoring, and retraining.
- Build feature pipelines and own the model lifecycle, including drift detection and eval.
- Partner with engineers; write production-quality, tested Python.

## Required
- 4+ years in data science or quantitative analysis.
- Strong experiment design and causal-inference fundamentals.
- Expert SQL.
- Proficient Python for modeling (pandas, scikit-learn, a gradient-boosting library).
- Experience taking at least one ML model to production — deployment, monitoring, and iteration.

## Nice to have
- Feature-store / ML-platform experience; MLOps tooling.
- Experience with ML system design for online serving.
- Software-engineering rigor: testing, code review, CI.

## Interview process
1. Recruiter screen (30 min).
2. Technical screen (60 min): SQL + a case on experiment design and a modeling problem.
3. Onsite (virtual): ML system design, a past-project deep-dive, an experimentation case, and behavioral.
