# Job description — Associate Product Manager

> Modeled on real public APM postings. Fictional company "Northstar".
> Pairs with persona `ops-to-pm` (a career switcher; this is intentionally a stretch).

**Northstar — Associate Product Manager**
Hybrid (Denver) · Product team

## About the role
Northstar builds workflow software for healthcare operations teams. We're hiring an Associate Product
Manager to own a slice of the product, work closely with engineering and design, and turn customer
problems into shipped features.

## What you'll do
- Own a product area: define the roadmap, write PRDs, and prioritize the backlog.
- Work daily with engineers and designers to ship features through discovery, build, and launch.
- Talk to customers, synthesize their problems, and translate them into product requirements.
- Define success metrics and use product analytics to measure impact and iterate.

## Required
- 2+ years of experience, ideally with some product, technical, or analytical exposure.
- Strong communication and stakeholder management.
- Comfort with data: can define metrics and reason about them; basic SQL a plus.
- Evidence of structured thinking and ownership of cross-functional outcomes.

## Nice to have
- Prior product-management experience or a transition from a closely adjacent role.
- Healthcare or workflow-software domain experience.
- Familiarity with discovery techniques, PRDs, and agile delivery.

## Interview process
1. Recruiter screen (30 min).
2. Hiring-manager screen (45 min): background + a product-sense discussion.
3. Onsite (virtual): a product-sense case, an analytical/metrics case, an execution case, and behavioral.
