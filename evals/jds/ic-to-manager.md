# Job description — Engineering Manager, Developer Platform

> Modeled on real public first-line EM postings. Fictional company "Aurora".
> Pairs with persona `ic-to-manager`.

**Aurora — Engineering Manager, Developer Platform**
Remote (US) · Developer Platform org

## About the role
Aurora's Developer Platform org builds the CI/CD, tooling, and infrastructure that every product team
relies on. We're hiring a first-line Engineering Manager to lead a team of 5–7 engineers — owning their
growth, the team's roadmap, and the delivery and health of the platform.

## What you'll do
- Manage and grow a team of 5–7 engineers: coaching, career development, performance, and hiring.
- Own the team's roadmap and delivery; partner with peer managers and product on priorities.
- Set and uphold technical direction with your senior engineers (without being the primary IC).
- Foster a healthy, inclusive team culture; run a fair performance and feedback process.

## Required
- Experience leading engineering work and growing engineers — formally or as a tech lead stepping up.
- Strong technical foundation in backend/platform systems (you can engage in design, not necessarily code daily).
- Track record of delivering through a team and aligning stakeholders.
- Clear communication and a people-first leadership approach.

## Nice to have
- Prior formal people-management experience (direct reports, reviews, hiring as hiring manager).
- Developer-platform / infrastructure domain experience.
- Experience driving reliability or developer-productivity programs.

## Interview process
1. Recruiter screen (30 min).
2. Hiring-manager screen (45 min): leadership background + management philosophy.
3. Onsite (virtual): a people-management case, a cross-functional/conflict scenario, a technical
   direction / design discussion, and a values interview. (Note: this loop is leadership-weighted, not a
   coding gauntlet.)
