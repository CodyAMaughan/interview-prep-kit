# Job description — Product Manager, Growth & Experimentation

> Modeled on real public growth-PM postings. Fictional company "Kestrel".
> Pairs with persona `growth-pm`.

**Kestrel — Product Manager, Growth**
Hybrid (Austin) · Growth team

## About the role
Kestrel is a consumer app scaling its growth engine. We're hiring a Product Manager for the Growth team
to own activation and retention — running a rigorous experimentation program and shipping the features
that move the funnel.

## What you'll do
- Own a growth area (activation/retention): set the roadmap, prioritize the backlog, write PRDs.
- Run a high-velocity experimentation program: design experiments, define guardrail metrics, and make
  ship/no-ship calls from results.
- Partner deeply with engineering, design, and data to ship and iterate.
- Define and own the metrics for your area; drive quarterly planning.

## Required
- 3+ years in product, growth, or a closely adjacent data/growth role.
- Strong experimentation fundamentals: A/B design, statistical literacy, guardrail metrics.
- Fluency with product analytics and SQL.
- Evidence of driving measurable funnel impact.

## Nice to have
- Experience owning a full product roadmap and backlog end to end.
- PRD craft and quarterly planning experience.
- Consumer / mobile growth experience.

## Interview process
1. Recruiter screen (30 min).
2. Hiring-manager screen (45 min): background + a growth/experimentation case.
3. Onsite (virtual): a product-sense case, an experimentation/analytics case, an execution/roadmap case,
   and behavioral.
